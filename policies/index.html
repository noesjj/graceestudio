Rental Agreement

This agreement is between Grace E. Studio and the Renter who is booking our studio space. By completing a booking, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

Payments & Deposits

All rental fees must be paid in full at the time of booking to confirm and hold your reservation. By signing this agreement, you authorize Grace E. Studio to charge your card for any damages, cleaning fees, or overage charges without additional consent.

Cancellations & Rescheduling

  • Cancellations made 48+ hours before session: Full refund
  • Cancellations made 24-48 hours before: 50% refund
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours before: No refund
  • Renter is granted one free reschedule with 48 hours notice
  • Reschedule must be within 1 month of original booking date
  • Grace E. Studio is not liable for building equipment failures, power outages, weather, acts of God, or emergencies
  • We reserve the right to cancel any booking, refund payments, and assume no further liability

Studio Rules

Violation of studio rules will result in a $500 minimum charge and immediate termination of rental with no refund.

  • DO NOT DRAG/SLIDE FURNITURE ON FLOORS. ALL FURNITURE MUST BE LIFTED.
  • Renter must be present for the entire duration of the rental
  • No smoking or vaping in the building or within 20ft of any entrance
  • Candle use is permitted with caution and must be extinguished before leaving
  • No food or drinks on or around furniture
  • No illegal drugs
  • Children must be supervised at all times
  • No pets allowed in The Joyce Loft. Pets are allowed in the Storefront studio as long as they are potty trained. The renter is responsible for any damaged to the studio or furniture from pets.
  • Maximum of 6 people in your party unless otherwise agreed
  • ABSOLUTELY NO GLITTER (including holiday decorations). $150 cleaning fee if used.
  • Studios have varying lighting conditions depending on time of day, weather, and season - no refunds for lighting dissatisfaction
  • Clean shoes are required - bring a clean pair to change into at the door
  • No muddy, dirty, or snowy shoes permitted in the studio

Rental Time & Overtime

  • Rental time begins and ends at your scheduled times
  • Your rental time includes setup and breakdown
  • If you are more than 20 minutes late, the rental and fee will be forfeited with no refund
  • Studio must be cleaned and vacated by the end of your rental period
  • Grace period of 5 minutes after your end time
  • After 5 minutes: $5 per minute overtime charge will apply
  • Early arrival or extended hours must be arranged and paid for in advance
  • No drop-off or pick-up of equipment outside rental time without prior arrangement

Cleaning & Trash

  • Leave the studio in the same condition and arrangement as you found it
  • All personal items must be removed by the end of your rental
  • Items left behind for more than 7 days will be discarded or kept by Grace E. Studio
  • Trash should be placed in provided bins
  • Minimum $50 cleaning fee if studio is not left clean and organized
  • You are responsible for all damages including floors, walls, windows, furniture, and equipment
  • Damage invoices must be paid within 5 business days

  • You are solely responsible for the conduct of all persons in your party
  • Grace E. Studio may have a representative present at any time
